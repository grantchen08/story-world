# Asset Specification: Sound Effects (SFX)

This document specifies the individual sound effects required.

## 1. Environmental
*   **`sfx_rat_gnaw.mp3`**: Scritch-scratch sound. "Crunch, crunch."
*   **`sfx_wind_forest.mp3`**: Whistling wind through pine needles.
*   **`sfx_boiling_water.mp3`**: Bubbling, rolling boil. "Roaring with sound."

## 2. Interaction
*   **`sfx_ui_select.mp3`**: Standard menu click.
*   **`sfx_item_get.mp3`**: Mystical chime (for finding the sword).
*   **`sfx_sword_swing.mp3`**: Sharp "Whoosh". The sword is invisible but cuts air.
*   **`sfx_dialogue_advance.mp3`**: Soft click/typewriter sound.
