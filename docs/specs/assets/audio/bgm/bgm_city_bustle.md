# Asset Specification: BGM - City Bustle

## 1. Description
Background music for the Capital City streets.

## 2. Technical Requirements
*   **File Name**: `bgm_city_bustle.mp3`
*   **Loop**: Yes.

## 3. Musical Direction
*   **Mood**: Busy, noisy, tense underneath. A hive of activity but with an undercurrent of fear/waiting.
*   **Instrumentation**: Drums, clatter/chatter textures, traditional strings (Guzheng) playing a repetitive, anxious melody.
*   **Reference**: "Streets were already bustling... great upheaval was coming."
