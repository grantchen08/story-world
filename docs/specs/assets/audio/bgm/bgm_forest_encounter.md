# Asset Specification: BGM - Forest Encounter

## 1. Description
Background music for the Cedar Forest.

## 2. Technical Requirements
*   **File Name**: `bgm_forest_encounter.mp3`
*   **Loop**: Yes.

## 3. Musical Direction
*   **Mood**: Mysterious, dangerous, supernatural.
*   **Instrumentation**: Low strings (cello/bass), slow tempo, significant silence/gaps. Occasional "shimmering" sound (dewdrops/phosphorescence).
*   **Reference**: "Every needle... hung with a dewdrop." "Two points of phosphorescent light."
