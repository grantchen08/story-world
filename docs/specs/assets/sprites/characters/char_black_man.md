# Asset Specification: The Black Man (Assassin)

## 1. Description
A mysterious, nihilistic figure. He is the catalyst for the climax and the executioner of the vengeance.

*   **Name**: Yan-zhi-ao-zhe (Self-proclaimed).
*   **Physical Features**:
    *   **General**: "Black clothes, black beard, black eyes, thin as iron."
    *   **Face**: "Cheekbones, eye sockets, and brow ridges stuck out high." "Wizened" but sharp.
    *   **Eyes**: "Two points of phosphorescent light" in the dark.
*   **Attire**:
    *   All black/dark grey.
    *   Carries a "round blue bundle" on his back later (containing the head).

## 2. Technical Requirements
*   **File Name**: `char_black_man.png`
*   **Sprite Size**: 32x48 or 48x48.
*   **Art Style**: Pixel art. Sharp angles, distinct silhouette.
