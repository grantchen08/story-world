# Asset Specification: Mei Jian Chi (Protagonist)

## 1. Description
Mei Jian Chi is the 16-year-old protagonist of *Forging the Swords*. He is characterized by his indecisive nature initially, which transforms into cold determination.

*   **Name**: Mei Jian Chi (The-One-With-A-Space-Between-His-Eyebrows)
*   **Age**: 16
*   **Physical Features**:
    *   **Face**: Defined by the "space between his eyebrows" (a wide glabella).
    *   **Eyes**: Like his father'sâ€”capable of "flashing like lightning" or "cold stars" when aroused, but often containing "infinite sorrow".
    *   **Build**: Youthful, not overly muscular but capable of physical labor (digging).
*   **Attire**:
    *   **Initial**: "Coat of indeterminate color" (ragged, poor village wear).
    *   **Quest Gear**: "Blue clothes" (dyed by his mother to match the sword). A "pure blue transparent sword" carried on his back.
