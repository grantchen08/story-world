# Asset Specification: The Mother

## 1. Description
Mei Jian Chi's mother is a tragic, weary, yet intensely determined figure. She carries the burden of vengeance and poverty.

*   **Role**: Quest Giver / Moral Anchor.
*   **Physical Features**:
    *   **Face**: Weathered, pale in the moonlight.
    *   **Eyes**: "Flashing in the dim white moonlight".
*   **Attire**:
    *   Simple, worn peasant clothes.
    *   Likely grey or brown tones.

## 2. Technical Requirements
*   **File Name**: `char_mother.png`
*   **Sprite Size**: 32x48 or 48x48.
*   **Art Style**: Pixel art. Consistent with Mei Jian Chi.
