# Asset Specification: Tileset - City Exterior

## 1. Description
The Capital city. A place of oppression, crowds, and the King's power. It contrasts with the quiet village.

## 2. Technical Requirements
*   **File Name**: `tiles_city_exterior.png`
*   **Grid Size**: 32x32.
*   **Style**: Oppressive, grey/black stone, dusty.

## 3. Key Elements

### Structures
*   **City Walls**: "Grey-black city walls and battlements". Tall, imposing.
*   **Gates**: Heavy wooden doors, iron reinforced.
*   **Streets**: Dust, dirt, crowded feeling.

### Environment
*   **Mulberry Tree**: "Big mulberry tree" outside the south gate (Save point/Rest area).
*   **Vegetable Stalls**: Baskets, scattered leaves.
