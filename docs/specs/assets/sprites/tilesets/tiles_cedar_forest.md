# Asset Specification: Tileset - Cedar Forest

## 1. Description
The transitional space between home and the city. A place of meeting and death.

## 2. Technical Requirements
*   **File Name**: `tiles_cedar_forest.png`
*   **Grid Size**: 32x32.
*   **Style**: Dark greens, shadows, mystical.

## 3. Key Elements

### Vegetation
*   **Cedar/Pine Trees**: Tall, dark, "needles hung with dewdrops".
*   **Moss**: "Head fell onto the moss".

### Atmosphere
*   **Dewdrops**: Sparkling in the dawn/moonlight.
*   **Phosphorescence**: "Two points of phosphorescent light" (Wolf eyes/Assassin's eyes effect).

## 4. Reference Quotes
> "Every needle of the cedar forest hung with a dewdrop."
> "Dark man steps out from behind a tree."
