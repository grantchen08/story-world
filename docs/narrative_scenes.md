# Narrative Scenes Breakdown

This document breaks down Lu Xun's *Forging the Swords* into playable game scenes. All descriptions and dialogue are direct quotations from the novel translation unless marked as [Adapted].

---

## Scene 1: The Rat and the Vat
**Location**: The Bedroom (Night)

**Environment**:
> "The pine torch burnt out. He stood silently in the dark, gradually seeing the moonlight's brightness."
> "The oil lamp flickers, casting long shadows against the mud walls." [Adapted]
> "A rat came out to gnaw at the pot lid, making a noise that grated on his nerves... crunch, crunch."
> "Suddenly, a splash startled him awake again. At the same time, he heard a scratching sound—claws against earthenware."

**Characters**:
*   **Mei Jian Chi**: "Sixteen years old. Nature is neither cold nor hot, changing not a bit."
*   **Mother**: "Sitting in the grey-white moonlight, her body seeming to tremble. The low voice contained infinite sorrow."
*   **The Rat**: "Wet black fur, bloated belly, worm-like tail... small sharp red nose gasping hurriedly—sniff, sniff."

**Objects**:
*   **Water Jar (Vat)**: "Little water left, so it couldn't climb out; it was just scratching along the inner wall."
*   **Pine Torch**: Used to "shine it into the water jar."
*   **Reed**: Pulled from the wall to "push the rat straight down to the bottom."

**Events**:
1.  **The Rat**: Rat gnaws at pot lid. Falls in. Mei Jian Chi lights torch. Torments it ("Serves you right!"). Pities it ("Suddenly felt pity"). Rescues it on reed. Disgusted by it ("Detestable"). Drops it back in. Kills it ("Stomped down. He heard a squeak").
2.  **The Awakening**: Mother wakes up. "Chi-er, what are you doing?"
3.  **The Vow**: Mother reveals the past.
    > "Your father was originally a master swordsmith... The King... extremely cruel... will surely kill me."
    > "A jet of white vapor rushed up... casting a peach-blossom glow over everything."
    > "Tears can never wash away fate."
4.  **The Dig**: Mother hands him a hoe.
    > "Dig!"
    > "At a depth of over five feet... rotten wood... coldness like touching ice and snow."
    > "The pure blue transparent sword appeared."

## Scene 2: The Cedar Forest
**Location**: Forest Path (Dawn)

**Environment**:
> "Every needle of the cedar forest hung with a dewdrop, hiding the night air within."
> "Dewdrops flashed with all kinds of brilliance, gradually turning into the colors of dawn."
> "Looking ahead, he could vaguely see the grey-black city walls and battlements."

**Characters**:
*   **Mei Jian Chi**: "Dressed in blue, the blue sword on his back."
*   **Mother (Memory)**: "Do not worry about me!" [Recall]

**Objects**:
*   **Blue Clothes**: "The length was just right... with the clothes and sword the same color, no one will be able to see it clearly."

**Events**:
1.  **Departure**: Leaving the house without looking back.
2.  **Transition**: Walking through the forest as night turns to dawn.

## Scene 3: The City Gate & Crowd
**Location**: City Streets (Morning)

**Environment**:
> "The streets were already bustling. Men stood around in rows; women peered out from doors."
> "Clouds of yellow dust."
> "People were packed tightly, craning their necks."

**Characters**:
*   **The Crowd**: "Swollen eyes, unkempt hair, and sallow faces without makeup." "Waiting anxiously yet patiently for this upheaval."
*   **Child**: "Suddenly ran over and almost bumped into the tip of the sword... making him break out in a cold sweat."
*   **Wizened-faced Youth**: "Gripped Mei Jian Chi's collar, refusing to let go, claiming his precious 'Dan Tian' had been crushed."
*   **The Dark Man**: "Black beard, black eyes, thin as iron. He said nothing, just smiled coldly." "Flicked the youth's chin."
*   **The King (Distant)**: "A fat man in painted clothes, with a grizzled beard and a small head. At his waist, one could vaguely see a blue sword."

**Objects**:
*   **Steamed Buns**: "Taking out two steamed buns to satisfy his hunger." (Acquired here).

**Events**:
1.  **The Procession**: Warriors, music, carriages. The King passes in a "large carriage with a yellow canopy."
2.  **The Attempt**: Mei Jian Chi tries to "step out through the gaps" but trips.
3.  **The Bully**: Encounter with the wizened-faced youth. Demands insurance.
4.  **The Rescue**: The Dark Man intervenes silently.
5.  **The Gossip**: Onlookers ask meaningless questions: "Age, address, and whether he had sisters at home."

## Scene 4: The Mulberry Tree
**Location**: Outside South Gate (Dusk)

**Environment**:
> "Wild and sparsely populated."
> "Big mulberry tree."
> "The sky grew darker... surroundings grew quieter step by step; he could hear his own breathing very clearly."
> "Two points of phosphorescent light—the dark man's eyes."

**Characters**:
*   **Mei Jian Chi**: "Eating buns... felt a sting in his nose."
*   **The Dark Man**: "Voice like an owl." "My soul bears so many wounds inflicted by others and by myself that I already hate myself!"

**Objects**:
*   **The Male Sword**: Carried on back.

**Events**:
1.  **The Wait**: Waiting for the King's return. Realizing he failed ("The King returned... by the east gate long ago").
2.  **The Proposal**: Dark Man appears. "Give me two things... one is your sword, the second is your head!"
3.  **The Motivation**: "I do not take vengeance for that reason... Yours is mine; he is me."
4.  **The Sacrifice**: Mei Jian Chi swings the sword from the nape of his neck. "His head fell onto the moss."
5.  **The Pact**: Dark Man kisses the "warm, dead lips twice."
6.  **The Wolves**: A pack of wolves attacks. Dark Man kills the leader ("Wave of the blue sword... wolf's head fell").
7.  **The Song**: Dark Man leaves singing: "Haha! Love, love, oh love! Love the blue sword..."

## Scene 5: The Royal Court
**Location**: Palace Hall / Golden Steps

**Environment**:
> "Golden cauldron filled with clear water and boiled with beast-charcoal."
> "Snow-white spray shot into the air in all directions."
> "Water was smooth as a mirror... reflecting many faces."

**Characters**:
*   **The King**: "Bored... furious. 'Alas! Boring!'"
*   **The Dark Man (Yan-zhi-ao-zhe)**: "Red-black like iron heated to a mild red."
*   **The Head (Child)**: "Fine eyebrows and long eyes, white teeth and red lips; a smile on its face."
*   **Queen/Concubines/Dwarves**: "Frozen expressions sprang to life... mixed with secret joy."

**Objects**:
*   **Golden Cauldron**: "Used for boiling oxen."
*   **The Blue Sword**: "Invisible blue sword, like a withered branch."

**Events**:
1.  **The Trick**: Dark Man presents the "child's head" trick.
2.  **The Dance**: The head sings and dances in the boiling water. "Reunion Dance."
3.  **The Lure**: "Must be done at the bottom." King approaches to look.
4.  **The Regicide**: Dark Man chops off the King's head. "Splash, the King's head fell into the cauldron."
5.  **The Battle of Heads**: "Bite hard on its ear... fighting to the death."
6.  **The Final Sacrifice**: Dark Man chops off his own head to assist.
7.  **The End**: Three heads sink to the bottom.

## Scene 6: The Funeral (Epilogue)
**Location**: City Streets (Funeral Procession)

**Environment**:
> "Road was already packed with men and women."
> "Altar tables appeared row by row."

**Characters**:
*   **Officials**: "Feigning sorrowful expressions."
*   **Common People**: "Knelt down... swallowed tears."

**Objects**:
*   **The Skulls**: "Blackness, whiteness, and size, they were all about the same."
*   **Golden Coffin**: Buries all three together.

**Events**:
1.  **The Confusion**: Fishing out the skulls. Trying to identify the King by "dragon nose-bridge" or "back-pillow bone."
2.  **The Decision**: Bury them all together.
3.  **The Irony**: The "treasonous rebels" enjoying the same rites as the King.
